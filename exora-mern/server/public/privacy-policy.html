Exora Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-29

Exora ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using our services, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: name, email, authentication identifiers, and organization info.
  • Usage Data: app interactions, device/browser metadata, IP address, timestamps.
  • Content You Provide: messages to the AI assistant, workflow selections, and configuration.
  • Third‑Party Integrations: tokens/identifiers necessary to connect services (e.g., Google, n8n).

How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Exora platform and dashboards.
  • Enable AI assistant features, recommendations, and workflow automations.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, LLM and automation infrastructure) under appropriate agreements and only as needed to operate the service.

AI & Automation

When you interact with the AI assistant or enable workflows, content may be processed by third‑party services to generate responses or execute automations. We apply access controls and retention policies to minimize data exposure.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service and as required by law. You may request deletion of your account or certain data, subject to legal or operational requirements.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Portability of data where applicable.

To exercise rights, contact us using the details below.

Security

We employ technical and organizational measures to protect data. No system is perfectly secure; please use strong passwords and enable available security features.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. We apply safeguards to protect your information consistent with this policy.

Children

Exora is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to our practices. We will post updates on this page and update the "Last updated" date.
